Rules

Ping Pong will count your number from 1 with the following exception:

  • Numbers that are divisible by 3 are replaced by "ping"
  • Numbers that are divisible by 5 are replaced by "pong"
  • Numbers that are divisible by 15 are replaced by "pingpong"
